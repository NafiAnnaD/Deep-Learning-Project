# Deep-Learning-Project

## Theme: 
Deep learning to foster safer online spaces

## Scope: 
The group mini-project aims to support a safer online environment by tackling hate speech in various forms, ranging from text and images to memes, videos, and audio content.

## Objective: 
To develop deep learning models that help foster healthier online interactions by automatically identifying hate speech across any of the diverse content formats. These deep learning models shall be carefully designed to prioritize accuracy and context comprehension, ensuring they differentiate between harmful hate speech and legitimate critical discourse or satire. 

## Context: 
Developing deep learning models that help prevent the surfacing of hateful rhetoric will lead to a more respectful online environment where diverse voices can coexist and thrive.
